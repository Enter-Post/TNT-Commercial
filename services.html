OUR SERVICES

TNT Commercial Services 615 offers a full suite of customizable services designed to meet your unique needs. As a proud locally owned company, we're committed to delivering excellence for our clients.

POWER + PRESSURE WASHING

TNT's commercial pressure washing restores and maintains the appearance of your property. We remove dirt, grime, oil stains, and buildup from sidewalks, building facades, parking areas, and entryways.

JANITORIAL

Our janitorial services deliver a clean, safe, and welcoming business environment. We provide customized cleaning programs including daily cleaning, floor care, sanitation, and specialty services.

GARAGE MAINTENANCE

TNT provides comprehensive parking garage maintenance including power sweeping, pressure washing, degreasing, paint restoration, high dusting, elevator cleaning, and more.

LANDSCAPING

Our landscaping services keep your property clean and inviting year-round. We offer lawn care, trimming, edging, debris removal, seasonal maintenance, mulching, planting, and leaf management.

INDUSTRIAL + COMMERCIAL WINDOW CLEANING

We deliver streak-free results for interior and exterior glass, removing dirt, fingerprints, and buildup. We also offer deep cleaning for tough debris and customizable schedules.

FACILITY HIGH DUSTING

Our high-dusting services target hard-to-reach areas where dust and debris collect—vents, beams, light fixtures, and ductwork—improving air quality and overall facility cleanliness.
